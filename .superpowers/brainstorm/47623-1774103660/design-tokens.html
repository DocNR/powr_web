Structural Rules

No-Line Rule
Zero 1px borders for sectioning. Use tonal shifting (base → card → elevated) + structural spacing + accent anchoring.
3pt Accent Bars
Every major content section gets a 3px left vertical bar in primary orange. Applied via border-l-[3px] border-l-primary.
12px Radius Everywhere
Cards, buttons, inputs, modals, sheets — all 0.75rem. No exceptions.
Button Hierarchy
Primary = gradient fill. Secondary = clear + 3pt left border. Ghost = no background.
